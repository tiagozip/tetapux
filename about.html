What is Tetapux?

Tetapux is a parody of modern-day internet scams, inspired by Hetapus and Hetapugs.

It includes a collection of fake phishing and adware pages, similar to the ones seen on these websites, including fake CAPTCHAS, gifts, and more. Tetapux is made to help teach how these websites work in order for you to protect yourself.
