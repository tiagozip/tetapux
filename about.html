What is Tetapux?

Tetapux is a parody of modern-day internet scams, adware and scareware, inspired by Hetapus and Hetapugs, ad networks managed by PropellerAds.

It includes a collection of fake pages, similar to the ones seen on these websites, including fake CAPTCHAS, fake gifts, and more. Tetapux is designed to help teach how these ad networks and scam websites work in order for you to protect yourself.

Warning: May include flashing lights and sounds. While this website is fully safe and does not download any dangerous files, It is not recommended to allow notifications when prompted to, since they can sometimes be annoying.
